# Company Choice

## 1. Company Selection

I choose **TrackFlow** as my company for the 24-week project.

TrackFlow is the best fit for me because its logistics context is highly practical and combines real operational complexity with clear automation opportunities. The company operates in two countries (United States and Spain), manages warehousing, last-mile delivery, and reverse logistics, and serves e-commerce clients that depend on speed and reliability. This makes the workflows data-rich and ideal for AI decisions, especially in routing and delivery coordination. I am specifically interested in the operational impact of route optimization because even small improvements can reduce delays, lower costs, and improve customer experience at scale. I understand this selection is final and cannot be changed later.

## 2. Departments of Interest

### Dispatch / Routing

I am most interested in Dispatch / Routing because poor route planning directly affects on-time delivery performance, fuel/operational cost, and customer trust. In a last-mile business like TrackFlow, better routing decisions are one of the highest-leverage improvements for daily operations.

### Customer Support

I am also interested in Customer Support because escalation delays can increase resolution time and reduce service quality for clients waiting on urgent shipment updates. Faster and more consistent escalations can improve communication, reduce friction, and support stronger customer relationships.

## 3. Automation / AI Challenge

### Selected challenge: AI Route Optimization

The AI challenge I want to focus on is route optimization for live delivery operations. This challenge is valuable because TrackFlow runs across two countries and depends on efficient routing to keep service levels high. An AI-driven routing layer can improve initial route assignment, adapt to disruptions in real time, and predict delay risk before failures occur. The result is better delivery performance, fewer incidents, and more reliable customer communication.

## 4. My AI Agent Idea

The agent acts as an intelligent routing assistant for dispatch teams. It recommends the best route plans before dispatch, continuously re-optimizes routes when disruptions happen, and predicts potential delays early.

It would need access to historical delivery data, real-time GPS/telematics, traffic and weather feeds, driver availability/capacity, and shipment constraints. It would integrate with the TMS, GPS tracking platform, maps/traffic APIs, and customer notification systems.

The agent would produce ranked route recommendations, delay-risk alerts, rerouting suggestions, and ETA updates for customer-facing teams. It would support automations such as dispatch change suggestions for approval, automatic delay notifications by email/SMS, and support ticket creation for high-risk deliveries.

Users would interact through an operations dashboard, while the agent communicates with other systems through APIs to keep routing, customer communication, and support workflows synchronized.
